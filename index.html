ABOUT RICH HOWARD

I'm a BBQ enthusiast and grilling guru based in NW Arkansas, passionate about sharing BBQ fun with fellow pitmasters. From food reviews to cooking tips, I've dedicated myself to helping others achieve BBQ greatness with the right tools, techniques, and dedication.

My mission is simple: test the best BBQ equipment and share honest reviews so you can make informed decisions for your own BBQ journey. Every product I recommend has been personally tested in my own backyard and proven to deliver results.

When I'm not smoking meat, you'll find me creating content, testing new equipment, and building the BBQ community one perfectly cooked brisket at a time. Follow along on Instagram and Threads for daily inspiration and behind-the-scenes BBQ fun!
